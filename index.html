People want good work, they want someone who know what they’re doing, but those are things that can be found in many places. What they value is empathy, someone who is patient and can explain to others what they’re doing, why it is important, and how that extra step will benefit them. From sharing valuable data for users and engineers to drawing up web page designs to clients, I’ve had experience explaining concepts to those not in my field and ensuring them that the work I do will benefit in the long run. What I plan to do is be transparent on what I’m doing and instill confidence in the consumer that my work is solid and that they will know why.
